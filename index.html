Marsoc Marines

Marsoc Marines are special forces. Their main specialty is the land. Marsoc Marines were involved on the war against terrorism. They are known to raid ships that have been captured by pirates. Marsoc Marines are one of the most elite counterterrorism units the U.S Military has to offer.

Marine Raiders

Marine Raiders mission is to recruit, train, sustain, and deploy scalable, expeditionary forces worldwide to accomplish special operations missions assigned by U.S. Special Operations Command (USSOCOM). To accomplish that, Marine Raiders equip and train Marines to succeed in austere conditions against a wide range of adversaries. Marine Raiders execute complex, distributed operations in uncertain environments, achieving silent success and strategic impact.
